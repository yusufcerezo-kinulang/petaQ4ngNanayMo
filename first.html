Editor's Note

Social media has become a big part of teenagers’ daily lives. Platforms like Facebook, Instagram, and TikTok allow young people to communicate, express themselves, and stay updated with trends around the world. It helps teenagers connect with friends, share ideas, and even learn new things through online content.

However, social media also comes with challenges. Teenagers may experience pressure from unrealistic standards, misinformation, or too much screen time. Because of this, it is important for young people to learn how to use social media responsibly and balance their online and real-life activities.

Author's Insight

As a teenager, I see how social media affects our daily lives. It can be a helpful tool for communication and learning, but it can also become distracting if we use it too much. I believe that teenagers should be aware of both the advantages and disadvantages of social media so that they can use it wisely. By being responsible online, we can enjoy its benefits while avoiding its negative effects.
